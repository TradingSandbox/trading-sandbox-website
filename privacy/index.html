Privacy Policy

How tradecli handles Google Sign-In identity, product analytics, error events, and account data.

Effective: June 1, 2026 Contact: contact@tradecli.in

Overview

tradecli is a terminal-based AI trading assistant for Indian markets. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it to operate, secure, and improve the product.

For questions or privacy requests, contact contact@tradecli.in.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google or use tradecli, we may collect:

  • Google account information, such as your email address, name, email verification status, and account identifier.
  • Approximate location and request metadata, such as country or region, when available.
  • Product usage events, such as CLI launch, login, logout, persona selection, setup status, skill category usage, app version, platform, install channel, and environment label.
  • Error events needed to diagnose failures, such as error type, command area, status code, and non-sensitive runtime context.

Information We Do Not Collect in Analytics

tradecli analytics are designed not to collect sensitive trading or local-machine details. Product analytics do not include:

  • Prompts, responses, chat transcripts, model context, or generated trading analysis.
  • Broker credentials, API keys, OAuth tokens, session cookies, or other secrets.
  • Portfolio holdings, order details, positions, trade history, watchlists, account balances, or personally sensitive financial records.
  • Full tool arguments, tool results, browser page contents, local file paths, or terminal command output.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and associate product analytics with signed-in accounts.
  • Operate, secure, debug, and improve tradecli.
  • Measure adoption, reliability, persona usage, skill usage, and setup friction.
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized access.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and compliance requests.

Google User Data

tradecli uses Google Sign-In to authenticate users and identify accounts. We request identity-related scopes such as OpenID, email, and profile. We do not request access to Gmail, Google Drive, Google Calendar, or other Google Workspace content.

We use Google user data only for authentication, account identity, product analytics, security, and support. We do not sell Google user data.

tradecli's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only in limited cases:

  • With Google, to provide Google Sign-In.
  • With providers that process information for us to operate, secure, and support tradecli.
  • If required by law, to protect users, or to protect the integrity and security of tradecli.

Retention

We retain account, product analytics, and error information for as long as reasonably needed to operate, secure, debug, and improve tradecli. We may aggregate or de-identify analytics for longer-term product measurement.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user information. No system is perfect, but we design collection and access controls to reduce unnecessary exposure.

Your Choices

You can stop using tradecli or sign out of the CLI at any time. To request access, correction, or deletion of account-linked information, contact contact@tradecli.in.

Changes

We may update this policy as tradecli changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
