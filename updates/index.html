What's happening with tradecli

A lightweight build log for what shipped, what changed, and what we're working on next.

Build Log

Recently shipped and what's next

June 15, 2026

AITradingOffice delivered v0.1.2

Delivered AITradingOffice as the office system of record for theses, forward tests, ledgers, account state, employees, clients, decisions, and reviews.

June 15, 2026

Office Mode agent desk v0.6.11

tradecli can launch CEO, Investor, and Trader workers as one office session from the interactive TUI. The mailbox coordinates delegation while AITradingOffice keeps the durable record behind the session.

June 15, 2026

Shared broker gateway v0.6.11

MCP-backed broker tools now run through a profile-scoped local gateway sidecar, so Office Mode panes share broker context instead of racing separate Groww or Zerodha Kite sessions.

May 2026

Trader workflow polish and broker integration work

Made the product more cohesive with actual trader workflows, explored additional broker integration paths, and started shaping PMS-style workflows around the same office model.

May 21, 2026

Technical Scanner v0.4.0

Discover trade candidates without naming a symbol first — ask Trader to scan the Indian market for volume spikes, pullbacks in an uptrend, oversold bounces, trend continuation, or breakout candidates, get a ranked shortlist back, and hand any pick straight into the trade workflow — so you can find opportunities instead of only analyzing ones you already had in mind.

Get the market read as a consistent brief instead of a loose list — daily and pre-market news now arrives in fixed sections: a dated headline with a bias call, the previous session's index closes, global cues, Nifty technical structure, sectors in play, volume leaders, FII/DII flow, and a scenario-based action plan — so you can scan the morning setup the same way every day and always know where to look.

May 15, 2026

Forward Test v0.3.17

Save and watch approved trade ideas from any strategy builder in a forward-test journal, track them alongside live positions, and review how each one played out with a daily scorecard — so you can validate edge before risking capital.

May 14, 2026

Browser-driven screener workflow v0.3.16

Replaced the older screener tools with a browser-driven workflow and added stricter guardrails, making screening sessions more reliable while keeping the flow safer around tool handoffs and automated actions.

May 12, 2026

Track-a-trade and learner workflow polish v0.3.15

Added live track-a-trade support, generic watch and cron flows in the TUI, improved free-text stock discovery with delegated Indian market news, and polished the persona migration, learner review, and workflow follow-up experience.

May 10, 2026

Investor and Portfolio Manager merged v0.3.14

Merged the Investor and Portfolio Manager personas into a single unified persona. Portfolio management is a shared concern across both traders and investors, so collapsing the two removes redundant workflows, simplifies navigation, and lets one persona handle position sizing, allocation, and long-term holdings end to end.

May 7, 2026

Learner UI and trading news workflows

Updated UI for learner module and enhanced trading news and thesis module.

May 4, 2026

Pine strategy and backtesting workflows

Tightened Pine strategy creation and backtesting and scripts.

Apr 27, 2026

News and thesis workflows v0.3.11

Added news and thesis functionality in Investor and Pro Trader modes.

Apr 26, 2026

Pro trader mode v0.3.10

Added pro trader mode with TradingView desktop app support and an improved UI for faster trader workflows.

Apr 11, 2026

Four personas mode v0.3.4

First release with four personas: Learner, Trader, Investor, and Portfolio Manager.
