# Learner Mode

Learner is a **guided curriculum**, not a free-form chat. It walks through chapters in a track (trading or investing), presents concepts, runs quizzes, and has you execute real workflows on your broker platform as interactive steps. Progress is saved automatically — if you switch away mid-step and come back, Learner resumes exactly where you were.

## How it works

- **Structured flow** — you follow chapters in order, not arbitrary prompts
- **Interactive steps** — the coach has you click through real broker UI, not just read
- **Broker-aware** — pick Groww or Zerodha and examples adapt to that platform
- **Quizzes** — concepts are checked before moving on
- **Pause/resume** — explicit control to stop a lesson and ask questions, then continue

## Typical prompts

```
Start chapter 1
Continue
/pause        (stop the lesson, just ask questions)
/continue     (resume from current step)
```

## Switching away and back

::: info Mid-step safety
If you jump to another persona mid-lesson, your step progress is preserved. When you return to Learner, you pick up from the same interactive step.
:::
