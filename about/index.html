About Us

Why tradecli exists

Indian retail traders have world-class brokers but fragmented tooling. tradecli is one terminal-based AI companion that learns with you, researches for you, and acts through the platforms you already use.

Vision

Understanding the system

Most people react passively to the systems that shape their lives — finance more than most. Charts, order books, liquidity, macro forces: all invisible mechanisms that determine outcomes, yet most participants only see the surface.

tradecli is built for the people who want to see the architecture. We believe understanding a market is what separates agency from reaction — being an actor, not a subject.

Our long-term vision is an environment that replicates the market at tick resolution — every signal, every flow, every regime shift — so traders can study how it actually works, not just how it looks.

Roadmap

Three layers

We're building this in three layers.

Layer 1 — Application (today). A terminal-first AI agent that drives the browser. We're shipping it now to learn what traders actually need, what the interface should feel like, and where the real friction is.

Layer 2 — Engine. A proprietary rules, data, and simulation layer on the backend. The tick-accurate market replica, the signals, the regime model.

Layer 3 — Agentic models. Our own agentic models, trained on financial datasets, specialized for trading workflows.

On data: we don't own market data, and aggregation is genuinely hard. So we lean on the browser — broker platforms, Screener, TradingView — as the source of truth. It's how we stay current without becoming a data vendor.

Team

Who's building this

A small team with a shared obsession — markets, systems, and the software that sits between them. We're keeping the team page lightweight for now; we'd rather let the product talk.
