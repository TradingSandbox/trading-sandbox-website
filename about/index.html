About

The trader is the operating system today.

tradecli exists to move that burden into a trader-controlled harness: agents that run on your tools, durable memory that compounds from your work, and guardrails that keep every action accountable.

Mission

Turn scattered market work into an agent-native system.

As a trader, you already hold the real system in your head: what you own, what you watch, why a thesis matters, which rule applies, and what changed since the last review. The software stack around you is powerful, but fragmented across brokers, charts, screeners, news, spreadsheets, and memory.

tradecli is the missing operating layer. It connects positions, watchlists, theses, journals, risk rules, browser context, broker actions, and outcomes through a local controller the trader owns.

The goal is not to replace the operator. It is to give the operator infrastructure: a system that preserves context, makes the workflow queryable, and lets the process scale beyond attention and memory.

Architecture

The OS, its agents, and the office.

The OS is the harness every agent runs on. It holds broker tools, browser tools, personas, mailbox coordination, sidecars, local services, and a single ladder of risk and action policy.

The agents are workers you spawn with a persona and mandate. One agent can research a thesis. Another can monitor a setup. Office Mode lets a coordinating seat delegate work to a visible desk of CEO, Investor, Trader, and PMS-style agents.

AITradingOffice is the system of record. It stores theses, forward tests, ledgers, journal entries, reviews, employees, clients, and account context so agent work does not disappear when a session ends.

Run a single persona and tradecli feels like a focused personal workflow. Spawn many agents against shared memory and it becomes the operating layer for a firm.

Guardrails

Autonomy has to climb a ladder.

Finance is not the place for vague autonomy. tradecli separates read-only context, suggestion, risk review, prepared orders, human approval, config-gated execution, audit trail, and post-trade review.

Execution is off by default and must be explicitly enabled per account. The system is built so trust is earned rung by rung, with the trader remaining the principal behind the capital.

That is why guardrails are part of the product shape, not compliance decoration. They are what make a desk of agents possible without pretending money is a toy.

India first

Built around the market stack traders actually use.

We are building for Indian public markets first because the workflow is specific enough that generic tools feel foreign: NSE and BSE conventions, broker behavior, product types, market hours, settlement, liquidity, and risk norms all matter.

The fragmentation is the opportunity. tradecli connects MCP-backed Groww and Zerodha Kite broker tools, TradingView, Screener, browser surfaces, and office memory into one operator-owned layer.
